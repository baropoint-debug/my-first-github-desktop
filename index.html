Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is Tapto.fan?

A: Tapto.fan is a platform where businesses and individuals can create their own AI chatbot and fan page (link collection homepage) for free. Upload your website URL, FAQ, and materials, and the chatbot is automatically generated with just a few clicks.

Q: What is a Fan Page?

A: A beautiful homepage with no-code ease, profile and background settings, and free link configuration combined with AI chatbot.

Q: What is the Membership Program?

A: The membership program allows creators to set a fee point (FP) per chat to earn revenue. Paid points are automatically deducted for conversations exceeding the free limit, with a portion credited to the creator.

Q: How do I set up Membership?

A: Free members: Up to 'N' free chats per day. Excess: Minimum 2 FP per chat, freely set. Revenue share by plan: Free Plan 4:6, Basic 5:5, Standard 6:4, Premium 7:3.

Q: How can I earn money?

A: Purchase paid points: 1 USD → 1,000 FP recharge. Deduct set FP per chat → Deducted amount credited to creator account. Withdrawal available when accumulated amount reaches $100 (100,000 FP).
